Tips for Setting and Achieving Realistic Goals

01

Start with Engaging and Accessible Material

Begin with books that are tailored to your interests and reading level. Choose engaging stories with relatable characters, vibrant illustrations, and simple language. This builds confidence and makes the reading experience enjoyable.

02

Establish a Consistent Reading Routine

Set aside a specific time each day for reading. Consistency is key when developing a reading habit. Whether it's before bedtime, during lunch breaks, or in the morning, having a routine helps make reading a natural part of your day

03

Explore Different Genres

Experiment with various genres to discover what resonates with you. Whether it's fiction, non-fiction, mystery, fantasy, or poetry, exploring diverse genres exposes you to different writing styles, perspectives , and ideas, broadening your literary horizons.

04

Set Realistic Goals

Set achievable reading goals. Start with shorter books or set a goal to read a certain number of pages each day. Celebrate your achievements, no matter how small. Gradually , you can increase the complexity and length of the books as your confidence and reading skills improve.
